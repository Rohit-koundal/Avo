Fitness instructors plan and instruct fitness sessions or classes. Fitness instructors may teach one-on-one or in group settings. The goal of a fitness instructor is to provide high quality exercise plans to clients seeking to improve their level of fitness. They assist clients in performing exercises by suggesting changes or useful techniques to maximize efficiency and minimize risk of injury.
